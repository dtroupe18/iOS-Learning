# Interval Runner

**Interval Runner** is a powerful, minimalist interval training app designed for iOS and watchOS. Create custom high-intensity interval training (HIIT) sessions, track your real-time performance on your wrist, and analyze your recovery with detailed post-workout heart rate graphs.

---

## 🚀 Features

* **Custom Interval Builder:** Easily configure warm-ups, work periods (run), rest periods (walk), and cool-downs.
* **Round Control:** Set a specific number of repetitions for your circuit.
* **Native watchOS App:** Leave your phone behind. Start, pause, and track your intervals directly from your Apple Watch.
* **HealthKit Integration:** Automatically syncs workouts to the Apple Health app, contributing to your rings and fitness trends.
* **Heart Rate Visualization:** View detailed, interactive line graphs of your heart rate zones after every session to see how hard you pushed.
* **Haptic Feedback:** Distinct vibrations on the watch to notify you exactly when to switch between running and walking without looking at the screen.

---

## 🛠 Tech Stack

* **SwiftUI:** For a modern, responsive user interface across iOS and watchOS.
* **HealthKit:** To access heart rate data and save workout metrics.
* **Charts (Swift Charts):** To render high-performance heart rate and pace visualizations.
* **WatchConnectivity:** For seamless syncing of workout templates between the iPhone and Watch.

---

## 📋 Requirements

* **iOS:** 18.0+
* **watchOS:** 11.0+
* **Permissions:** HealthKit access (requested upon first launch).
